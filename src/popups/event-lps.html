Join LPS for fun activities at the entrance to the Henrich Preserve which is one of the LPS nature preserves along the WWII Veterans Trail. You will be able to make a personalized button about nature in Norton and enjoy other autumn-inspired activities. The entrance is a short way down the trail from the Johnson Preserve on N. Washington.

LPS is an all-volunteer, nonprofit organization dedicated to PRESERVING THE WILD PLACES AND WILD THINGS IN NORTON, MA.
